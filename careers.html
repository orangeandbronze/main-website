Recruitment and Selection Process and FAQs

Interested to work in Orange and Bronze? Please read the FAQs to learn more about O&B's screening and selection process.

How can I apply?

Choose from the job vacancies posted on the website. If you see a vacancy that fits your profile, submit your application by clicking the
(Apply for this Position) button on the job posting.

What happens after I submit my application?

A pre-employment assessment will be sent via email. You must complete the assessment within twenty-four (24) hours of receipt.

In case you do not receive the online assessment within twenty-four (24) hours, notify us at careers@orangeandbronze.com.
We will send another email to make sure you receive the information.

What are the pre-employment assessments for job and internship applicants?

  • Software Engineering positions - online programming exam. The applicant may choose his/her preferred programming language.
  • Quality Engineer, Business Analyst, Project Manager, Software Tester, or Systems Administrator positions:
    1. Online English and Math Proficiency Tests
    2. Online Technical exam specific to the position applied for - only applicants who pass both the English and Math Proficiency Tests will be invited to take the technical exam.
  • Applicants for Business Support positions (HR, Sales, and other administrative positions) - online English and Math Proficiency Tests. Only applicants who pass both will be invited for interviews.

How will I know if I passed or failed the exam/interview?

You will be notified at every stage of the application process via call or email.
Results will be released within a week of the final stage of the selection process.

How does your recruitment and selection process work?

  1. O&B’s HR team evaluates applications based on the profile and results of the pre-employment assessment.
  2. Shortlisted applicants will be invited to the O&B Headquarters for technical and behavioral interviews. In some cases, an applicant may be invited for an interview by O&B clients.
  3. O&B makes an offer to a qualified applicant after the required interviews.

How long does it take for an applicant to get hired?

A qualified applicant may receive an offer within 2-3 weeks from the receipt of the application.

Who should I reach if I have more questions?

Send an email to careers@orangeandbronze.com
